    Mortgage-backed exposures

    Used in the following modules

    • CRE

    Legal references

    • OFRnew
      • Art.72-72g
      • Appendix 3 chi.3

    Process

    • Loan-to-value (LTV) computed in table core_positions_calc_ltv by property, i.e. by entry in list_properties.
    • Assign mortgage certificate/contract to exposures in core_positions_crm_ltv. It defines the ReportingCategory (REstateSelfResi,REstateOthResi,REstateSelfCom,REstateOthCom) and risk weight of the positions or portions of position guaranteed by real estate.
    • Only then comes the actual CRM (according to the definition of OFR Art.61 al.1 let.b,c and d) which is independent from the LTV calculation and visible in the table core_positions_crm_ca_b4
      • In order to preserve the information regarding the LTV splits in the CRM, we have added the fields ExsposureLtvLoadId and ExposureLtvId in this table.

    Risk weighting

    • The risk weight derived from the LTV or counterparty's risk weight as per the appendix 3 chi.3 of the OFR is computed in the field core_positions_crm_ltv.CALTVRiskWeight. This risk weight is sometimes referred below as .
      • The counterparty's risk weight computed according to the appendix 2 of the OFR is computed in the field core_positions_calc.CARiskWeightB4
    • The risk weight which will be applicable to the position or portion of position is computed in the field core_positions_crm_ltv.CARiskWeightB4.

    • Below, the rules applied to computed the field core_positions_crm_ltv.CARiskWeightB4, which paraphrases the articles of the OFR related to this topic:

      • Subordinated AND the RW associated to the LTV is not the lowest one of its category according to appendix 3 (art.72c al.6)
      • Land & construction loans
        • For residential property - Own-use (appendix 3 chi.3.1) where requirements of art.72c al.1 let.b-f are met (art.72e al.2)
        • For residential property - Own-use (appendix 3 chi.3.1) where requirements of art.72c al.1 let.b-f are not met (art.72e al.2)
          • (see above)
        • For residential property - Other, where requirements of art.72c al.1 let.b-f are met (art.72e al.3) and
        • For residential property - Other, where requirements of art.72c al.1 let.b-f are not met (art.72e al.3) or
        • For commercial property - Own-use
          • (see above)
        • For commercial property - Other

    Relevant input fields for corresponding concepts/notions

    • Own-use → list_properties.UseOf = 'PU'
    • Residential property → list_properties.TypeOf in ('Condo','SFH','MFH')
    • Commercial property → list_properties.TypeOf not in ('Condo','SFH','MFH')
    • The field core_module_specifics.CALTVDefinedRWFlag defines whether the requirements of art.72c al.1-3 are met or not. Essentially, if the requirement are met, the risk weight is defined by the LTV, hence the name of the field.
    • Land/Construction loan → core_positions.SubType in ('MortgageLoanCons','MortgageLoanCons-ETP','LandLoan')

    Loan-to-value (LTV) calculation

    • (art.72a al.1)
      • TBD: The pledged cash which can decrease the numerator is pledged cash which enter an asset-liability netting agreement and can be recognized as: core_positions.NettingAgreementType='NAL' and core_positions.PledgedFlag = 1 and core_positions.Type = 'Account' and core_positions_calc.CAEligibilityFlag = 1
      • If this cash is used here, it should not be used during the CRM again
    • The LTV is computed per pledged property
      • If multiple pledges for one credit, use an appropriate distribution key to distribute the total credit amount to each pledge so that an LTV can be computed for each pledge (art.72a al.2)
